Akan Name Generator

This is an Akan Name generator,
that takes a user's birthday,
calculates the day of the week they were born
and outputs their akan name.


Akan names are derived from Ghanian culture.
Frequently in Ghana, children are given their first name
as a 'day name' which corresponds to the day in the week they were born.
